# Game Concept

## Working Title
LexiQuest

## Short Pitch
LexiQuest is a small 2D educational game where the player moves through a level, collects correct English words, and avoids or fixes incorrect choices. The vertical slice will focus on one polished level with clear feedback, simple rules, and a complete start-to-finish experience.

## Target Player Experience
The player should feel that they are learning vocabulary through quick decisions rather than reading a static quiz. The game should be approachable, readable, and easy to restart.

## Core Mechanic
The player navigates a 2D level and interacts with word objects. Correct answers reward progress or score. Incorrect answers give clear feedback and may reduce health or time.

## Planned Vertical Slice
- One playable level
- Player movement and collision
- Word collection or question interaction
- Score, health, or timer UI
- Win and lose states
- Basic sound and visual feedback
- Short testing log showing improvements

## Scope Control
The project will avoid multiplayer, large open worlds, complex AI, and many levels. The goal is a small but complete and stable game.

## Legal, Ethical, Social, Accessibility, and Security Notes
- Use original assets, Unity built-in assets, or clearly credited free assets.
- Avoid collecting personal data.
- Use readable fonts, clear contrast, and simple controls.
- Include a restart option and clear player feedback.
